# Students-Grades-prediction-
In this project, I worked with a student dataset. The data involves students taking two exams—G1 and G2—with the goal of predicting their final result, G3. This dataset presents a challenge because student grades are not determined solely by the available numerical data; they also depend on factors that are difficult to quantify, such as a student's state of mind or personal circumstances. To address this, I employed Ridge and Lasso regression models to achieve the most accurate prediction possible, though you could certainly extend the work by experimenting with other regression models.

You can see the original data here: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/tejas14/student-final-grade-prediction-multi-lin-reg/code
